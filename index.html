About Me

Hello! I'm Maruf, a fourth-year undergraduate studying Computer Science at Williams College, currently based in New York City. With a strong foundation in Python, Java, JavaScript, algorithms, and object-oriented programming, I have honed my skills over three years of combined experience in full-stack web development, DevOps, and big data analytics.

My passion lies in AI software engineering, where I strive to create innovative solutions that bridge the gap between complex algorithms and user-friendly applications. Throughout my academic journey, I've been actively involved in various projects and research initiatives that have allowed me to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world problems.

At Williams College, I have excelled in my coursework, particularly in Data Structures, Algorithms, Machine Learning, and Web Development. I have also taken on leadership roles, serving as Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador and participating in numerous hackathons and AI research projects.

Beyond academics and work, I am an avid photographer and enjoy capturing the beauty of everyday moments. I also share my knowledge through blog posts and YouTube videos, aiming to inspire and educate others in the field of computer science.

I am constantly seeking opportunities to leverage my skills and contribute to projects that make a meaningful impact. Let's connect and explore how we can collaborate on exciting tech ventures!
